Lord Krishna

*--------****--------*

Lord Krishna played various roles in his earthly form like a mentor, deity, teacher, friend, lover, and more. Lord Krishna holds a flute in his hand and wears the feather of a peacock on his forehead. He was born to kill the evil king of Mathura, known as Kansa, and freed the devotees from his dreadfulness. Lord Krishna is popular for his teachings of supreme knowledge assembled in Hindus' holy book known as “Shrimad Bhagwad Gita”. Lord Krishna followed the path of “ Dharma ”.
